Marvin Chancán

I am currently a Ph.D. student in robotics & autonomous systems at QUT advised by Professor Michael Milford. Previously, I received a B.S. in mechatronics engineering, M.S. in mechanical engineering, and MBA in project management.

My research interest is at the interface of computational neuroscience, computer vision, machine learning and reinforcement learning; developing large-scale visual place recognition, localization, mapping and navigation systems for mobile robots and autonomous vehicles.
