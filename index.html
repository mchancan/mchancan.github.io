Marvin Chancán

mchancanl AT uni.pe

Welcome! I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in robotics and autonomous systems at QUT, advised by Prof. Michael Milford. Previously, I received my B.Sc. in mechatronics engineering from UNI, and worked in the automation industry for over 4 years.

In my research, I focus on the intersection between neuroscience and machine learning, with the aim of developing algorithms and techniques for robots and autonomous vehicles.
