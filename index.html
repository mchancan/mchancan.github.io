Welcome! I am currently a PhD student in electrical engineering and robotics at QUT, advised by Prof. Michael Milford. Previously I received my BSc in mechatronics engineering from UNI and also worked in the automation and IT industry for over 6 years.

In my research, I focus on the intersection between neuroscience, machine learning, and neural networks, with the aim of developing faster learning algorithms and techniques for robots and autonomous vehicles.
