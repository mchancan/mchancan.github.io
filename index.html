Marvin Chancán

I am a Ph.D. candidate in robotics and autonomous systems at QUT advised by Professor Michael Milford. Previously, I received a B.S. in mechatronics engineering and M.S. in mechanical engineering.

My current research interests include neuroscience, reinforcement learning, and machine learning, with applications in large-scale visual localization and navigation for mobile robots and autonomous vehicles.
